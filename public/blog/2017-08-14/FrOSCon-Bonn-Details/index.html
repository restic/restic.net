Meeting at FrOSCon 2017 - Details

14 Aug 2017

As announced a few weeks ago we’re meeting at this year’s FrOSCon on 19 August 2017, 15:00 CEST in Sankt Augustin near Bonn/Cologne in Germany (free entry)! We’ll get a project room for the afternoon, the room number will be announced as an update to this blog entry, on Twitter and in the forum.

It’d be nice to know how many of you will be able to join us, so please leave a comment in the GitHub issue #1110 or in the forum. We’re looking forward to meeting you all, thanks!
